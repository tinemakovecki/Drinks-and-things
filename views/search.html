Search form

This is the search for finding drink suggestions. It isn't difficult to use. You simply select the ingredients of the meal you want to match in the "Meal type" settings. The "Preferences" section contains additional options for how the results are shown and sorted as well as which drinks are suggested. After you've made your choice you press the "Search" button and browse the drinks that would go well with your food.

For example, if you're going to make lasagna, you would select the Red Meat option in the "Meat type" section as well as the Nightshades in the "Vegetables" section. You might want to also select Herbs in the "Spices" section or the sort of cheese you will include to find a more specific match. If you want more information about a category you can hold your mouse over the text to see examples. If there are no results found, you might want to go back and select less ingredients, focusing only on the most important ones. Have fun!
